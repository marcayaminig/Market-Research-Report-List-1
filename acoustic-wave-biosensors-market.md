<p><strong>The Acoustic Wave Biosensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Quartz Crystal Microbalance (QCM) Sensor</li><li>Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Acoustic wave biosensors utilize sound waves to detect biological interactions, primarily through two key types: Quartz Crystal Microbalance (QCM) sensors and Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) sensors. QCM sensors measure mass changes on a quartz crystal's surface, providing high sensitivity for detecting biomolecular interactions. In contrast, SAW sensors utilize surface acoustic waves to monitor changes in material properties, enabling real-time detection of analytes. Both technologies are crucial in diagnostics, environmental monitoring, and food safety applications, enhancing sensitivity and specificity in biosensing.</p></p>
