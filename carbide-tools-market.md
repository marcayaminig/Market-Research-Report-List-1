<p><strong>The Carbide Tools Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Aluminium Carbide</li><li>Calcium Carbide</li><li>Silicon Carbide</li><li>Tungsten Carbide</li><li>Iron Carbide</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carbide tools market encompasses various types, each serving distinct industrial applications. Aluminium carbide enhances aluminum processing and grain refinement. Calcium carbide is used in acetylene production and as a desulfurizing agent. Silicon carbide is favored for its hardness and thermal conductivity, often used in abrasive materials. Tungsten carbide, known for its exceptional toughness, is widely utilized in cutting tools and industrial machinery. Iron carbide, meanwhile, is often involved in metallurgical processes, adding strength to iron-based alloys.</p></p>
