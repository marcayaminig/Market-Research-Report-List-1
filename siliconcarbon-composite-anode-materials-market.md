<p><p>Silicon/Carbon Composite Anode Materials are advanced materials used in lithium-ion batteries to enhance energy density and performance. By combining silicon's high capacity for lithium storage with carbon's structural stability, these composite anodes address key challenges such as volume expansion and cycle life degradation that pure silicon anodes typically face. This hybrid approach improves overall battery efficiency, making them ideal for applications in electric vehicles, consumer electronics, and renewable energy storage.</p><p>The Silicon/Carbon Composite Anode Materials Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.1% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for high-energy density batteries and technological advancements in electric mobility.
