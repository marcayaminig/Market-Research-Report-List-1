<p><ul><li>Homofermentative</li><li>Heterofermentative</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The silage inoculation reagent market comprises two main types: homofermentative and heterofermentative inoculants. Homofermentative inoculants primarily produce lactic acid, enhancing fermentation efficiency and lowering pH, which aids in preserving forage quality. In contrast, heterofermentative inoculants create varied fermentation products, such as acetic acid and ethanol, offering benefits like improved aerobic stability and enhanced nutrient availability. Both types play crucial roles in optimizing silage production, ensuring better feed quality, and increasing livestock productivity.</p></p>
