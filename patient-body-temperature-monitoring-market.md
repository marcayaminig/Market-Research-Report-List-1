<p><ul><li>Non-Invasive Temperature Monitoring</li><li>Invasive Temperature Monitoring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The patient body temperature monitoring market encompasses two primary types: non-invasive and invasive temperature monitoring. Non-invasive temperature monitoring involves methods such as infrared thermometers, tympanic devices, and skin patches that measure temperature without penetrating the body, making them convenient and comfortable for patients. In contrast, invasive temperature monitoring requires the insertion of devices, like thermocouples or intravascular sensors, to provide precise core temperature readings. Both methods are crucial in clinical settings for accurate patient assessment and treatment management.</p></p>
