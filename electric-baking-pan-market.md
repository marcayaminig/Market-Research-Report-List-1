<p><p>The electric baking pan market is segmented into two primary types: unilateral and double-sided. Unilateral electric baking pans have a single heating surface, making them ideal for tasks like grilling or toasting one side at a time, offering simplicity and ease of use. In contrast, double-sided electric baking pans feature heating elements on both sides, allowing for faster cooking and even heat distribution, suitable for preparing items like sandwiches or pancakes, enhancing versatility and efficiency in cooking.</p></p>
