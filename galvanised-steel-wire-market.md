<p><p>Galvanised steel wire is a type of wire that has been coated with a layer of zinc to enhance its resistance to corrosion and rust. The resulting wire is widely used in various applications, including construction, agriculture, fencing, and industrial sectors, due to its durability and strength.</p><p>The Galvanised Steel Wire Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.2% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand in construction and manufacturing industries.
