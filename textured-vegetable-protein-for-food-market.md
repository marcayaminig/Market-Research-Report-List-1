<p><p>Textured Vegetable Protein (TVP) is a plant-based protein derived from soybean flour, widely used in the food market. It is available in two primary forms: protein concentrate and protein isolate. Protein concentrate retains a higher proportion of natural fiber and contains about 60-70% protein, making it ideal for products aiming to retain some nutritional value. Protein isolate, on the other hand, is processed to remove most fats and carbohydrates, boasting over 90% protein content, perfect for health-focused formulations. Both types cater to varying consumer preferences for nutrition and texture.</p></p>
