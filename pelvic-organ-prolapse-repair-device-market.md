<p><p>The Pelvic Organ Prolapse Repair Device market primarily includes vaginal pessaries and vaginal mesh types. Vaginal pessaries are non-surgical, removable devices inserted into the vagina to support pelvic organs and alleviate symptoms of prolapse. In contrast, vaginal mesh is a surgical solution, providing structural support to the pelvic floor by implanting a mesh material during surgery. Both options cater to varying patient needs, with pessaries offering a conservative approach, while meshes are designed for more severe cases requiring surgical intervention.</p></p>
