<p><p>The low-density polyurethane foam market comprises two main types: soft low-density PUR and rigid low-density PUR. Soft low-density PUR is characterized by its flexibility and cushioning properties, making it ideal for applications in furniture, bedding, and automotive seating. In contrast, rigid low-density PUR offers enhanced structural support and insulation, suitable for construction materials, refrigeration, and packaging. Both types leverage their unique properties to cater to diverse industries, driving demand for lightweight, durable, and energy-efficient solutions.</p></p>
