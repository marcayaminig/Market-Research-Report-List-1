<p><p>The B2B digital payment market is categorized into two types: cross-boundary and non-cross-boundary. Cross-boundary markets involve transactions between businesses located in different countries, necessitating currency conversions and compliance with international regulations. Non-cross-boundary markets, on the other hand, involve transactions between businesses within the same country, typically simplifying payment processes as they operate under the same legal and regulatory frameworks. Both markets leverage digital platforms to enhance efficiency and streamline the payment process, catering to diverse business needs.</p></p>
