<p><p>The paperless bushings market primarily includes two types: resin impregnated fibre bushings and resin impregnated synthetic bushings. Resin impregnated fibre bushings are made from natural or synthetic fibers infused with resin, offering excellent electrical insulation and mechanical strength, ideal for harsh environments. Meanwhile, resin impregnated synthetic bushings utilize advanced synthetic materials that enhance durability and reduce maintenance needs. Both types are designed to eliminate the use of traditional paper insulation, promoting environmental sustainability and improving performance in electrical applications.</p></p>
