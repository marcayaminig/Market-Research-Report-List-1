<p><p>The heart stent market encompasses several types, each with distinct characteristics. Drug-eluting stents release therapeutic drugs to prevent artery re-blockage, improving long-term outcomes. Bare metal stents provide structural support without additional coatings, suitable for patients needing faster recovery. Bioactive stents incorporate materials that promote healing and reduce inflammation. Radioactive stents deliver localized radiation to inhibit cell proliferation, minimizing restenosis risk. Each type is tailored to specific patient needs and clinical scenarios, influencing treatment decisions and market dynamics.</p></p>
