<p><p>The side-entry agitator market includes two main types: fixed angle design and automatic swivel option. Fixed angle agitators maintain a consistent, pre-set angle for mixing, which ensures uniformity in processes but can limit flexibility. In contrast, automatic swivel agitators offer adaptability by changing angles during operation, improving efficiency and mixing effectiveness. Both types cater to various industrial applications, such as chemical processing and wastewater treatment, optimizing performance based on specific mixing requirements and operational conditions.</p></p>
