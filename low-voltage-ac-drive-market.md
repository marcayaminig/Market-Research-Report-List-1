<p><strong>The Low Voltage AC Drive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Voltage Source Inverter (VSI)</li><li>Current Source Inverter (CSI)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The low voltage AC drive market is primarily categorized into two types: Voltage Source Inverter (VSI) and Current Source Inverter (CSI). VSI converts DC to AC voltage, enabling better control over motor speed and torque, making it ideal for variable frequency applications. In contrast, CSI provides a stable current output, suitable for high-power applications, where maintaining current integrity is crucial. Both types serve various industries, facilitating energy-efficient operations and enhanced performance in electric motor control systems.</p></p>
