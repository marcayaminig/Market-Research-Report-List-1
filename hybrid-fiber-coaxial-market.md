<p><p>The Hybrid Fiber Coaxial (HFC) market encompasses various components essential for telecommunications. Transceivers facilitate data transmission, while amplifiers boost signal strength. Encoders and modulators convert data into formats suitable for transmission over different media. RF combiners merge signals, and splitters distribute them to multiple outputs. Optical nodes serve as connection points for fiber and coaxial systems, while fiber optic cables provide high-speed data transfer. Coaxial/copper cables support legacy systems and deliver broadband services. Together, these components enhance network performance and efficiency.</p></p>
