<p><p>The aluminum and steel rolling oils market comprises two primary types: cold rolling oils and hot rolling oils. Cold rolling oils are used in the cold rolling process, providing lubrication and cooling to reduce friction and wear during metal deformation. They help achieve precise thickness and surface finish. On the other hand, hot rolling oils are utilized in high-temperature applications where they prevent oxidation and enhance the flow characteristics of molten metal. Both types are essential for optimizing metalworking efficiency and product quality.</p></p>
