<p><p>The dietary chocolate products market consists of three main types: white chocolate, dark chocolate, and milk chocolate. White chocolate, made from cocoa butter, offers a creamy texture but lacks cocoa solids, resulting in a sweeter flavor. Dark chocolate, rich in cocoa content, is often hailed for its health benefits, including antioxidants. Milk chocolate combines cocoa solids, milk, and sugar, providing a balance of sweetness and creaminess. Each type appeals to different consumer preferences, influencing their market dynamics and demand.</p></p>
