<p><p>The Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT) market is categorized into three main types: Wired Technology, Wireless Technology, and Field Technology. </p><p>Wired Technology utilizes physical connections, offering stable and high-speed data transmission, ideal for fixed environments. </p><p>Wireless Technology enables flexible connectivity, allowing devices to communicate without cabling, enhancing ease of installation. </p><p>Field Technology encompasses devices and sensors used directly in production environments, focusing on monitoring and controlling industrial processes, ensuring operational efficiency and real-time data analytics. Together, they enhance industrial automation and data-driven decision-making.</p></p>
