<p><strong>The Emerging Inkjet Printing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Demand Inkjet Printing Technology</li><li>Continuous Inkjet Printing Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The emerging inkjet printing market is primarily divided into two types: Demand Inkjet Printing Technology and Continuous Inkjet Printing Technology. Demand Inkjet Printing, also known as drop-on-demand, releases ink droplets only when needed, making it ideal for high-quality prints and variable data applications. In contrast, Continuous Inkjet Printing constantly ejects ink, allowing for high-speed printing suitable for large-scale production, such as packaging or labeling. Both technologies cater to diverse industrial needs, enhancing customization and efficiency in printing solutions.</p></p>
