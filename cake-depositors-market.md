<p><p>Cake depositors are specialized equipment used in the baking industry to precisely dispense batter or dough into molds. The single-piston depositor operates with one piston, allowing for straightforward and consistent portion control, ideal for smaller production runs or artisan products. In contrast, the multi-piston depositor employs multiple pistons to create higher throughput, making it suitable for large-scale operations that require efficient, high-volume dispensing. Both types enhance accuracy and efficiency in cake production, catering to diverse market needs.</p></p>
