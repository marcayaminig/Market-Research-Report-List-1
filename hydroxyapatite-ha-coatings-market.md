<p><p>The Hydroxyapatite (HA) coatings market is segmented into thin and thick HA coatings. Thin HA coatings generally range from a few micrometers to several tens of micrometers in thickness, offering enhanced surface bioactivity and improved bonding to implants. They are commonly used in applications like dental and orthopaedic implants. In contrast, thick HA coatings, which can exceed hundreds of micrometers, provide superior mechanical support and are often utilized in load-bearing applications. Both types enhance osseointegration and promote tissue healing.</p></p>
