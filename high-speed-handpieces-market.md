<p><strong>The High Speed Handpieces Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inside Exhaust Handpiece</li><li>Outside Exhaust Handpiece</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-speed handpieces market is primarily categorized into two types: inside exhaust handpieces and outside exhaust handpieces. Inside exhaust handpieces expel air and debris through internal channels, providing enhanced visibility and reducing contamination risks during dental procedures. In contrast, outside exhaust handpieces direct exhaust air externally, which can offer better cooling but may require additional suction systems to maintain a clean working area. Each type serves distinct preferences and operational needs in dental practices, influencing overall efficiency and patient comfort.</p></p>
