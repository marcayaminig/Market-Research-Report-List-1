<p><strong>The Computer CPU Cooling Fans Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial Fans</li><li>Centrifugal Fans</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CPU cooling fans market primarily consists of axial and centrifugal fans, each serving distinct cooling needs. Axial fans, characterized by their blades that push air parallel to the fan shaft, are commonly used in desktops for their efficiency in moving larger volumes of air at lower pressures. In contrast, centrifugal fans use a rotating impeller to draw air in and expel it at right angles, providing higher pressure and efficiency in compact spaces, making them ideal for high-performance systems.</p></p>
