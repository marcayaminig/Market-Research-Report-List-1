<p><p>The standalone patient portal market can be categorized into two primary deployment types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based deployment involves hosting the portal on remote servers, providing easy access to patients and healthcare providers from any device with internet connectivity. This model offers scalability, reduced IT maintenance, and lower upfront costs. On-premise deployment, in contrast, involves installing the portal software on local servers within a healthcare facility, offering enhanced control over data security but requiring more significant investments in infrastructure and IT support.</p></p>
