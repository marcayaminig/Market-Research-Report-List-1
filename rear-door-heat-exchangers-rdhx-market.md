<p><p>Rear Door Heat Exchangers (RDHx) are cooling solutions utilized in data centers to manage heat generated by IT equipment. The two primary market types are active and passive RDHx. Active RDHx utilize fans or pumps to enhance airflow and cooling efficiency, while passive RDHx rely on natural convection and conduction without mechanical assistance. Both types aim to maintain optimal operating temperatures, reduce energy consumption, and improve overall system reliability in data center environments.</p></p>
