<p><ul><li>Wavelength Dispersive (WDXRF)</li><li>Energy Dispersive (EDXRF)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>XRF analyzers, used for elemental analysis, primarily fall into two types: Wavelength Dispersive X-ray Fluorescence (WDXRF) and Energy Dispersive X-ray Fluorescence (EDXRF). WDXRF offers high-resolution measurements and is ideal for trace element analysis in complex matrices, while EDXRF provides faster analyses and is suited for bulk materials and where speed is critical. Both technologies have unique advantages, catering to different applications across industries such as materials science, environmental monitoring, and quality control.</p></p>
